❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free quotes?
Yes — all quotes are completely free with no obligation. We'll visit your site, assess the area, and give you a clear, itemised quote with honest pricing before any work begins.
How quickly can you start?
This depends on our current schedule and the size of your project. We'll discuss availability during the quoting process. For urgent jobs, contact us directly by phone to check our earliest availability.
What areas do you service?
We are based in Veldrif and Laaiplek and service the full West Coast region — including St Helena Bay, Hopefield, Langebaan, Paternoster, Saldanha, Vredenburg, and surrounding farms. Call us if you're unsure about your area.
What information do you need for a quote?
It helps to know your location, the type of paving you want, the approximate size of the area (in square metres if possible), and what the area is currently like. Don't worry if you're unsure — we'll guide you through it during the site visit.
Do you prepare the base before paving?
Absolutely. A properly compacted, prepared base is the foundation of all good paving. We never cut corners on base preparation — it's what prevents sinking, cracking, and uneven surfaces over time.
Can you handle large commercial projects?
Yes. We have experience with residential driveways, business forecourts, industrial yards, schools, and farms. No job is too big or too small — get in touch and we'll assess your project and provide a competitive commercial quote.
What payment methods do you accept?
Payment terms and methods are discussed and agreed upfront during the quoting process. We work transparently — no surprises. Contact us to discuss your specific project and we'll outline everything clearly.
How long does paving last?
Quality paving that has been properly installed on a good base can last 20–30 years or more. The key is correct foundation preparation and using good quality materials — both of which are standard practice at Paving Master Construction.
