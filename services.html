Block Paving

Block paving offers outstanding strength and flexibility. The individual concrete blocks interlock to create a surface that moves slightly under load — making it more resistant to cracking than poured concrete.

We use this method for residential driveways, business forecourts, industrial yards, and public spaces where a clean, professional finish is required alongside heavy-duty performance.

Heavy-duty surface for vehicles and forklifts
Flexible under load — resistant to cracking
Clean, professional finish for businesses
Multiple pattern and colour options available

Interlock Paving

Interlock paving uses specially shaped blocks that lock together without mortar — creating a surface that is incredibly strong yet flexible. Used extensively in industrial sites, loading zones, and high-traffic commercial areas.

The precision-engineered interlocking system means weight is distributed across multiple blocks simultaneously, making this the top choice for heavy-duty applications.

Engineered for heavy-duty industrial use
No mortar required — locks under compression
Handles heavy truck and forklift loads
Excellent drainage through the joints
Long service life with minimal maintenance
